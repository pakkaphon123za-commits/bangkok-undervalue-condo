# Bangkok Condo Market Brief

> A data-driven look at where Bangkok condos trade below their modeled value — and why proximity to transit matters.

**Live interactive map:** [pakkaphon123za-commits.github.io/bangkok-undervalue-condo](https://pakkaphon123za-commits.github.io/bangkok-undervalue-condo/)

## TL;DR

Out of **9,599 condo listings** near Bangkok’s mass-transit stations, **6.91%** are priced below what a hedonic distance-decay model predicts. The best hunting ground is the **BTS Sukhumvit Line**: it has the steepest price decay with distance (−26.2% per km) and the largest share of undervalued units. Stations like **On Nut**, **Bearing**, and **Bang Na** stand out as value corridors.

## How this was built

1. **Scraped** Fazwaz condo listings with coordinates, price, area, bedrooms, bathrooms, and listed date.
2. **Mapped** every BTS/MRT/SRT/Airport Rail station from OpenStreetMap.
3. **Matched** each listing to its nearest station and computed straight-line distance.
4. **Modeled** log price-per-sqm as a function of distance, area, line, and interchange status — separately per transit line.
5. **Flagged undervalued** listings as those whose residual fell below the global median by more than the median absolute deviation (MAD).

> **Methodology note:** A listing is marked undervalued when its log-price residual is below the global median residual of `0.013085` using MAD `0.297672`. This controls for location, distance to station, floor area, and unit characteristics.

## Key findings

### 1. Sukhumvit Line dominates both premiums and discounts

- **8.85%** of Sukhumvit Line listings are undervalued (387 units).
- Price drops **−26.2% per km** from the station — the sharpest decay in the network.
- Value corridors appear from **On Nut to Bearing**, where older stock and large supply create pricing gaps.

## What to watch next

Three upcoming extensions are likely to reshape relative pricing:

- **MRT Orange Line** — east-west corridor through central Bangkok.
- **MRT Purple Line South** — extension toward southern suburbs.
- **SRT Dark Red South extension** — deeper connection into the outer south.

Stations near these corridors that already show high undervaluation could re-rate once the lines open.

## Caveats

- Distances are straight-line, not walking time.
- Undervaluation is model-relative, not an absolute investment recommendation.
- Lines with few observations (Pink, Dark Red, Light Red) have wider uncertainty.
